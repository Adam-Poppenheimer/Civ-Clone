The CityConfig type exposes the following members.

Properties
  NameDescription
BaseGrowthStockpile
The base amount of food required to grow a city's population. Used by PopulationGrowthLogic.GetFoodStockpileToGrow
FoodConsumptionPerPerson
The amount of food that each citizen consumes every turn.
GrowthPreviousPopulationCoefficient
A coefficient that modifies the food required to grow a city's population. Used by PopulationGrowthLogic.GetFoodStockpileToGrow
GrowthPreviousPopulationExponent
An exponent that modifies the food required to grow a city's population. Used by PopulationGrowthLogic.GetFoodStockpileToGrow
HurryCostPerProduction
The amount of gold it costs per production to hurry a project. Currently unused.
MaxBorderRange
The maximum distance from which a city can acquire new tiles.
MinimumSeparation
The minimum distance that must be maintained between any two cities.
PreviousTileCountCoefficient
A coefficient that modifies the cost of acquiring new tiles. Used by BorderExpansionLogic.GetCultureCostOfAcquiringTile
PreviousTileCountExponent
An exponent that modifies the cost of acquiring new tiles. Used by BorderExpansionLogic.GetCultureCostOfAcquiringTile
TileCostBase
The base cost of all tiles. Used by BorderExpansionLogic.GetCultureCostOfAcquiringTile
UnemployedYield
The yield of all unemployed citizens.
