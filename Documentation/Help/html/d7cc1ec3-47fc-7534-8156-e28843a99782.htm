IIncomeModifierLogic Methods

The IIncomeModifierLogic type exposes the following members.

Methods
  NameDescription
GetRealBaseYieldForSlot
Gets the real base yield of a particular slot, taking into account any improvements or base modifiers that might be altering its yield.
GetYieldMultipliersForCity
Retrieves the yield modifiers for all income generated by the given city, disregarding other modifiers from that city's civilization.
GetYieldMultipliersForCivilization
Retrieves the yield modifiers for all income generated by cities belonging to the given civilization.
GetYieldMultipliersForSlot
Retrieves the yield modifiers that are applying specifically to the slot in question, disregarding other modifiers from that slot's city or its civilization.
