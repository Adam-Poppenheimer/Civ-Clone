ICity Properties

The ICity type exposes the following members.

Properties
  NameDescription
ActiveProject
The project the city is currently trying to construct.
CellBeingPursued
The cell that the city is actively seeking to acquire via border expansion.
CultureStockpile
The amount of culture currently stockpiled in the city.
FoodStockpile
The amount of food currently stockpiled in the city.
LastIncome
The income the city generated during the last call to PerformIncome.
Location
The IHexCell where the city is placed.
Population
How many citizens the city has to assign to slots.
ResourceFocus
The current resource focus of the city, which determines what tiles the city will prioritize when assigning its citizens to slots
transform
The transform that controls the city's orientation in physical space.
