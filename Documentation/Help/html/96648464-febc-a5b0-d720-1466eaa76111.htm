Methods
  NameDescription
PerformDistribution
Redistributes all of the city's citizens amongst its available slots, as determined by the city's ResourceFocus.
PerformExpansion
Performs a single turn's worth of expansion. This should control the value of CellBeingPursued and slowly add new territory to the city if it's generating a sufficient amount of culture.
PerformGrowth
Performs a single turn's worth of growth and starvation logic, increasing or reducing the city's population if it's met the conditions for growth or starvation.
PerformIncome
Performs a single turn's worth of income, resetting LastIncome and modifying CultureStockpile and Foodstockpile.
PerformProduction
Performs a single turn's worth of production, progressing through the ActiveProject and executing it should it complete.
SetActiveProductionProject(IBuildingTemplate)
Establishes a new ActiveProject that, when completed, creates the building of the argued template in this city.
SetActiveProductionProject(IUnitTemplate)
Establishes a new ActiveProject that, when completed, creates a unit of the argued template in this city.
