IIncomeModifierLogic
Class that calculates the income modifiers and base yield for a variety of entities. These are used to determine the real yield of a particular slot by a particular city belonging to a particular civilization.
IResourceGenerationLogic
Determines the total resource yield generated by a city, including changes due to modifiers. Performs a similar task for individual slots and for unemployed citizens.
